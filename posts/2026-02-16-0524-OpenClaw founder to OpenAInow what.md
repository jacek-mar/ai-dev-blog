Sam Altman announced it on X: Peter will “drive the next generation of personal agents,” and OpenClaw will “live in a foundation as an open source project that OpenAI will continue to support.” Let me say this clearly: Peter earned this. He built something almost 200,000 people have starred on GitHub.
