Today at the Microsoft Build conference, Satya Nadella announced an expanded collaboration with Hugging Face, to make its wide diversity of open models easy to deploy on Azure secure infrastructure.
If you head over to Azure AI Foundry today, you will find a vastly expanded collection of 10,000+ Hugging Face models you can deploy in a couple clicks to power AI applications working with text, audio and images.
