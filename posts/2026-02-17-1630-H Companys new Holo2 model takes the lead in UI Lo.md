Two months since releasing our first batch of Holo2 models, H Company is back with our largest UI localization model yet: Holo2-235B-A22B Preview. This model achieves a new State-of-the-Art (SOTA) record of 78.5% on Screenspot-Pro and 79.0% on OSWorld G. Available on Hugging Face, Holo2-235B-A22B Preview is a research release focused on UI element localization.
