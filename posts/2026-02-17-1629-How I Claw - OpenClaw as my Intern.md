Everyone’s excited about OpenClaw. The security implications are real. People are buying Mac minis to run it, giving an autonomous agent unfiltered access to their accounts, SSH keys, passwords — their whole life. OpenClaw’s value does come from accessing your email, calendar, and messages. But I think you can get most of the value without handing over the keys to your life.
