## About AI Summaries

Summaries are generated automatically using Claude (Anthropic) via KiloCode Cloud Agent webhooks.

- Summaries may be incomplete or contain inaccuracies.
- Summaries do not substitute for reading the original article.
- Original quotes are reproduced verbatim and attributed to their source.
- All summary pages include a visible notice: *"Summaries are AI-generated. Please read the original article for full context."*

---

## robots.txt and Scraping Policy

This scraper:

- Identifies itself with a descriptive `User-Agent` header.
- Respects `robots.txt` directives before fetching any page content.
- Fetches only publicly accessible pages (no login-gated content).
- Stores only article metadata and text — no images or binary assets.
- Links back to the original source on every summary page.

If you operate one of the indexed sources and wish to opt out, open an issue at:
https://github.com/jacek-mar/ai-dev-blog/issues
